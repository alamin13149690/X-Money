❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🔐 Q: I can't log in. What should I do?

A: Make sure your email and password are correct. If forgotten, use the password reset option on the login page.

💰 Q: My deposit is not showing. Why?

A: Deposits may take 5–10 minutes to reflect. If delayed, contact support with your transaction ID.

👑 Q: How do I upgrade my VIP level?

A: You can upgrade manually from the VIP page or earn upgrades through referrals and loyalty bonuses.

🔗 Q: How does the referral system work?

A: Submit a valid referral code once to earn a bonus. The referrer also gets rewarded. You can’t refer yourself.

🎁 Q: Can I claim daily bonuses?

A: Yes! Visit the bonus page once every 24 hours to claim your reward.

📱 Q: Is there an Android app?

A: Yes! Visit the download page to get the latest APK and installation guide.

🛟 Q: How do I contact support?

A: Go to the support page for 24/7 help via WhatsApp or email.
